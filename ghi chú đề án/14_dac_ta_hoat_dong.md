# 14 - Dac ta cach thuc hoat dong (Use cases)

## UC-01 Tra cuu tu
- Tac nhan: Nguoi dung.
- Mo ta: nhap tu khoa -> he thong tim kiem -> hien chi tiet.
- Luong chinh:
  1. Nguoi dung nhap tu.
  2. He thong goi API /words/search.
  3. Neu co ket qua, mo chi tiet tu.

## UC-02 Xem chi tiet tu
- Tac nhan: Nguoi dung.
- Mo ta: xem nghia, vi du, dong nghia.
- Luong chinh:
  1. Nguoi dung chon mot tu.
  2. He thong goi /words/id/{id}.
  3. Hien noi dung.
