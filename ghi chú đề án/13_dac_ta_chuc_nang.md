# 13 - Dac ta chuc nang ung dung (SRS tom tat)

## 1. Muc tieu he thong
- Xay dung ung dung tu dien Tieng Viet – Viet phuc vu tra cuu va goi y tu vung cho nguoi viet noi dung.
- Cung cap cong cu quan ly du lieu tu vung va dong nghia cho admin.

## 2. Pham vi
- Tra cuu tu vung, nghia, loai tu.
- Goi y dong nghia theo muc do lien quan.
- Hien thi thanh ngu, tuc ngu lien quan.
- Tra cuu tu theo chu de.
- Quan tri tu vung (them, xoa, sua, cap nhat du lieu).
- Quan tri nghia va vi du.
- Quan tri dong nghia va tuc ngu.

## 3. Doi tuong su dung
- Nguoi dung cuoi: tac gia, hoc sinh, nhan vien van phong.
- Admin: quan ly kho tu vung va noi dung.

## 4. Chuc nang chinh (Functional Requirements)

### FR-1 Tra cuu tu
- Dau vao: tu khoa.
- Dau ra: thong tin tu (loai tu, nghia, vi du).

### FR-2 Goi y dong nghia
- Dau vao: tu da tra cuu.
- Dau ra: danh sach dong nghia co muc do.

### FR-3 Hien thi thanh ngu/tuc ngu
- Dau vao: tu da tra cuu.
- Dau ra: danh sach thanh ngu/tuc ngu lien quan.

### FR-4 Tra theo chu de
- Dau vao: chu de.
- Dau ra: danh sach tu thuoc chu de.

### FR-5 Quan tri du lieu (Admin)
- Them/xoa/sua tu.
- Them/xoa/sua nghia.
- Them/xoa/sua vi du.
- Them/xoa/sua dong nghia.
- Them/xoa/sua tuc ngu.

## 5. Yeu cau phi chuc nang (Non-Functional)
- Hieu nang: tra cuu nhanh trong thoi gian ngan.
- Bao mat: han che truy cap admin.
- Tinh mo rong: co the them chu de, loai tu, truong thong tin.
- Tuong thich: Windows desktop va Android.

## 6. Giao dien
- App nguoi dung: giao dien mobile style, ton mau xanh – tim.
- App admin: dashboard + danh sach quan ly.

## 7. Rang buoc
- Backend chay tren Render Free (co the sleep).
- Database SQLite.
