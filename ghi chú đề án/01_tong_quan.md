# 01 - Tong quan de an

## Muc tieu
- Xay dung app tu dien Tieng Viet – Viet phuc vu tra cuu, dong nghia, thanh ngu, tuc ngu, chu de.
- Co 2 ung dung tach biet: app nguoi dung va app admin.
- Backend API phuc vu chung cho 2 app.

## Doi tuong su dung
- Content Creator, copywriter, nha van.
- Hoc sinh, sinh vien.
- Nhan vien van phong.
- Nguoi nuoc ngoai hoc tieng Viet nang cao.

## Pham vi chuc nang
- Tra cuu tu vung, nghia, loai tu.
- Goi y dong nghia theo muc do lien quan.
- Hien thi thanh ngu, tuc ngu lien quan.
- Tim theo chu de (cam xuc, giao thong, am thuc, ...).
- Quan tri du lieu tu vung tren app admin.

## Dau ra he thong
- API backend (FastAPI) chay online.
- Database luu tru (SQLite).
- App nguoi dung (Windows va Android).
- App admin (Windows).
