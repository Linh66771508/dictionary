# 16 - Mo ta he thong va kien truc

## 1. Mo hinh kien truc
- Client (Flutter) <-> Backend (FastAPI) <-> Database (SQLite)

## 2. Thanh phan
- App nguoi dung: tra cuu va hien thi.
- App admin: quan tri du lieu.
- Backend API: xu ly logic, truy van DB.
- DB: luu tu, nghia, dong nghia, tuc ngu.

## 3. Hoat dong tong quan
- Client gui request HTTP.
- Backend truy van DB va tra JSON.

## 4. Diem manh
- Don gian, de trien khai.
- Co the mo rong sang DB khac neu can.
